Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how data is handled on the jsonxml.pl website, available at https://jsonxml.pl.

1. Personal data

The jsonxml.pl website does not collect, process, or store any personal data of its users. All JSON ↔ XML conversions are performed locally in your web browser and are not transmitted to any servers.

2. Cookies

The website may use cookies to ensure proper operation and display of advertisements. Cookies are small text files stored on the user’s device, which may be read by Google systems (e.g., Google AdSense).

3. Google AdSense advertising

This site may display advertisements from the Google AdSense network. Google may use cookies such as DoubleClick to personalize ads.

More information about how Google uses data can be found at: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

You can disable personalized Google ads at: https://adssettings.google.com/

4. Statistics and analytics

The website does not use analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics). No data about user traffic or visit history is collected.

5. Data security

The website operates over a secure HTTPS connection, ensuring that data transferred between the user’s browser and the server is encrypted.

6. External links

The site may contain links to other websites. The owner of jsonxml.pl is not responsible for the privacy policies or content of those external sites.

7. Changes to this policy

The administrator reserves the right to update this privacy policy. The current version of the document is always available at https://jsonxml.pl/en/privacy-policy.html.

8. Contact

For questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact: kontakt@jsonxml.pl
